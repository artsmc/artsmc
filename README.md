Hi 👋 My name is Mark Campbell
==============================

Engineer and Co-founder
-----------------------

My experience includes building design systems for companies such as Wunderman and developing an application for companies such as NGA (National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency), P&G, Pfizer, the DOD(Department of Defense) DISA and many other has generated a strong sense of web design and development standards. Along with being recognized as the best web designer in DC by Thumbtack (2014), shows a strong sense of personal success. Having built a startup in the Dreamit Ventures portfolio and working on marketing services for small businesses has contributed greatly to a unique skill set with a focus on application development, user experience, and its relation to data.

* 🌍  I'm based in Washington D.C
* ✉️  You can contact me at [mark@melvill.app](mailto:mark@melvill.app)
* 🚀  I'm currently working on [Melville Technology LLC](http://www.melville.app/)
